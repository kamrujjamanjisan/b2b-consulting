Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most important questions from our clients.

"Why is our validation taking so long?"

In 90% of cases, it's about wrong focus: Time is spent on non-critical documentation (screenshots of standard functions, over-documentation of low-risk features) while truly critical aspects – the ones the auditor wants to see – are neglected. This results from lack of experience. We know exactly where to look: critical processes thoroughly, non-critical ones efficiently.

"How quickly can you start – and how fast are you finished?"

Start: For urgent projects within 1-2 weeks. For Go-Live Rescue: 48h for initial analysis. Duration: Depends on scope, but our goal is always 50% faster than industry average. Example: A standard SaaS validation that normally takes 6 months, we complete in 3.

"Do you work on-site or remote?"

Both. We typically start with an on-site kickoff (1-2 days) to understand the team, system, and context. The actual work then happens hybrid: remote for documentation and tests, on-site for reviews, workshops, and go-live. For international projects, we work 90% remote – we've perfected this across 60+ systems in 8+ countries.

"What sets you apart from other CSV consultants?"

Experience + Pragmatism. We're not documentation machines billing by the day. We deliver results: your system goes live. Our approach – Performance System Validation – focuses on what really matters. Critical processes are thoroughly tested, non-critical ones handled efficiently. This saves time and money without creating compliance risks.

"The FDA/EMA requires documented evidence for everything!"

Myth. The FDA released the CSA Guidance (Computer Software Assurance) in 2022. It explicitly calls for fewer screenshots and more Critical Thinking. GAMP 5 2nd Edition goes in the same direction. Our approach is closer to current regulations than many old SOPs that still rely on "document everything."

"Do we really need external help? We have QA."

Depends. If your QA has capacity, knows the systems (SAP, Veeva, cloud tools), and has experience with CSA/Risk-Based Validation – do it internally. If at least one of these points is missing, you'll save time and avoid costly mistakes with us. We integrate into your team – we don't replace it.

"You're more expensive than the freelancer we usually use."

Per day, maybe. But the freelancer asks every day what to do. They bill hours. We deliver results. We're finished in 3 months while the freelancer is still formatting documents after 9 months. Do the math on who's more expensive in the end – including your own time for coordination.

"Can you also train our team?"

Yes. We offer workshops on CSA, Risk-Based Validation, and system-specific topics (SAP, Veeva, etc.). But the best knowledge transfer happens through collaboration: when we run a project together, your team learns our methodology automatically. After 2-3 projects, you'll be significantly more self-sufficient.
